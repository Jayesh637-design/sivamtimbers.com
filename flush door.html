ABOUT FLUSH DOOR

A flush door is a type of interior door that has a smooth and flat surface on both sides. It is called a "flush" door because its surface is even with the door frame, creating a seamless appearance when closed. Flush doors are widely used in both residential and commercial buildings due to their simplicity, versatility, and aesthetic appeal.
