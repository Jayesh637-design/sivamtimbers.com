ABOUT PLYWOOD

Plywood is a versatile building material widely used in various applications due to its strength, durability, and cost-effectiveness. It is made from thin layers of wood veneers, known as plies or laminae, which are glued together with their grains oriented perpendicular to one another. This arrangement of layers gives plywood its characteristic strength and stability. we have two types of plywood. it comes with two type HARD WOOOD & NEEM WOOD
